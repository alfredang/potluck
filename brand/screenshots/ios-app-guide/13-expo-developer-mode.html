iOS Developer Mode

Learn how to enable Developer Mode on your iOS device to run development builds.

⚠️ Required for Development

Starting with iOS 16, Apple requires Developer Mode to be enabled on devices to run development builds. This is a security feature that must be manually enabled.

How to Enable Developer Mode

1 Connect Your Device
Connect your iPhone or iPad to your Mac using a USB cable. Open Xcode and select your device.
2 Open Settings
On your iOS device, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management.
3 Enable Developer Mode
Tap on the developer app from your trusted provider, then toggle Developer Mode to ON.
4 Restart Your Device
After enabling Developer Mode, you'll need to restart your device for the changes to take effect.

✅ Ready for Development

Once Developer Mode is enabled, you can run development builds, use Expo Go, and test on physical devices.

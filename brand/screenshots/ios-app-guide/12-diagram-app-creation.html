App Creation Flow

🍎 Apple Developer Account
Enroll in the Apple Developer Program ($99/year) to get access to certificates, provisioning profiles, and App Store Connect.
1
🆔 Register Bundle ID
Create a unique bundle identifier in Apple Developer portal (e.g., com.potluck.app). This identifies your app.
2
📜 Create Certificates
Generate a Distribution Certificate in Apple Developer portal to sign your app for App Store distribution.
3
📋 Create Provisioning Profile
Create an App Store provisioning profile linking your certificate and bundle ID.
4
⚡ EAS Build
Run EAS Build to compile your React Native app with Expo. Credentials are managed by EAS.
eas build --platform ios
5
🛍️ App Store Connect
Upload your IPA to App Store Connect, add app metadata, screenshots, and submit for review.
6
✅ Live on App Store
After review (1-3 days), your app is live and available for download worldwide!

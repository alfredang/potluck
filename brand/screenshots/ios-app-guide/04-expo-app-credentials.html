App Credentials

Learn about what app credentials Android and iOS require. Expo automates the process of signing your app.

iOS Credentials

📜
Distribution Certificate

The distribution certificate is associated with you as a developer, not a particular app. You may only have one distribution certificate per Apple Developer account. Used for all your apps.

Required $99/year
📋
Provisioning Profiles

Tied to both your distribution certificate and a specific bundle ID. Specifies which devices can install your app. Must be recreated if certificate expires.

Required App-specific
🔑
Push Notification Keys

Required for sending push notifications to users. Each key can be used for all your apps. Apple Push Notification service (APNs) requires these keys.

Optional For Push

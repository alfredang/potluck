# PotLuck Chef Acquisition Marketing Plan

## Target
- **Goal:** 100 chefs in 90 days
- **Offer:** Free listing for 3 months

## Marketing Best Practices

### 1. Lead with Value, Not Sales
- Focus on benefits for chefs (more customers, no fees)
- Testimonials from early chefs
- Success stories

### 2. Multi-Channel Approach
- **Social Media:** Instagram Reels, TikTok videos showing cooking
- **WhatsApp:** Direct outreach to home cooks
- **Content:** Blog posts, recipes, chef features

### 3. Phased Launch
- **Week 1-2:** Soft launch with existing network
- **Week 3-6:** Social media campaign
- **Week 7-12:** Influencer partnerships
- **Week 13-90:** Scale and optimize

### 4. Key Messages
- "List your food for FREE"
- "Reach more customers"
- "Turn your passion into income"

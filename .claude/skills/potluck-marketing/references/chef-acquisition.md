# Potluck Chef Acquisition Marketing Plan

> Moved from the former root `CHEF_MARKETING_PLAN.md` into the **potluck-marketing** skill.
> Supply-side growth playbook: recruiting home chefs onto the marketplace.

## Target
- **Goal:** 100 chefs in 90 days
- **Offer:** Free listing for 3 months (then tiered subscription, SGD)

## Marketing Best Practices

### 1. Lead with Value, Not Sales
- Focus on chef benefits: more customers, keep more of what you earn, no commercial kitchen needed
- Testimonials and success stories from early chefs (e.g. "earning ~$2,000/month from home")

### 2. Multi-Channel Approach
- **Social Media:** Instagram Reels + TikTok videos showing the cooking and the chef's story
- **WhatsApp:** Direct outreach to home cooks (+65 9048 0277)
- **Content:** Blog chef-spotlights and recipes (the site's chef-spotlight posts double as
  recruitment proof — each links to a real `/chef/{slug}` profile)
- **On-site:** `/become-chef` funnel + the homepage enquiry form ("Become a home chef" interest)

### 3. Phased Launch
- **Week 1–2:** Soft launch with existing network
- **Week 3–6:** Social media campaign
- **Week 7–12:** Influencer / food-creator partnerships
- **Week 13–90:** Scale and optimise

### 4. Key Messages
- "List your food for FREE"
- "Reach more diners in your neighbourhood"
- "Turn your passion into income"
